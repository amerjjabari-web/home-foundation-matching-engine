# Home Foundation (مؤسسة البيت) - Matching Engine

## Overview
Home Foundation is a hybrid mental health platform addressing the "silent crisis" in Jordan. This repository houses the logic for our **AI-Powered Peer Matching Engine**, designed to bridge the gap for the 25% of Jordanians experiencing mental health symptoms.

## The Challenge
- **Scarcity:** Fewer than 1 mental health professional per 100,000 people in Jordan.
- **Stigma:** High barriers to seeking traditional clinical help.
- **Vulnerability:** Targeting youth (12-24) and the 750,000+ refugees in the region.

## Our Solution
The engine uses Machine Learning to match users with Peer Supporters based on:
1. **Lived Experience:** Shared history (e.g., addiction recovery, displacement).
2. **Linguistic/Cultural Alignment:** Specific dialects and community contexts in Amman.
3. **Risk Stratification:** Real-time identification of "red-flag" needs for clinical escalation.

## Open Source Commitment
We believe mental health support is a human right. This project is developed as a **Digital Public Good** to be replicated across the MENA region and other low-resource environments.
